## What Chamelo is

Chamelo is a smart eyewear brand that pioneered the tint-adjustable and color-changing lens category. We use proprietary liquid crystal film technology to give wearers direct control over their lenses — change tint or color instantly, manually, automatically, or via app.

Named a **Time Magazine Best Invention**. We sit at the intersection of performance sport and lifestyle, with premium pricing and a technology-first identity.

We don't sell sunglasses. We sell **control** — over light, over environment, over how you see the world.

## Mission (long)

At Chamelo, we're engineering a new era of smart eyewear by combining state-of-the-art technology with cutting-edge style. Our mission goes beyond creating dynamic and stylish glasses — we're pushing the boundaries of what's possible in a traditionally static industry.

Our products are designed to be as functional as they are fun and effortlessly cool. We're constantly innovating, developing advanced yet easy-to-use technology that allows for an unprecedented level of customization and versatility.

At the core of Chamelo is our passionate community. By listening to our users and incorporating their feedback, we ensure our eyewear reflects real needs from real people. This user-centered approach drives everything we do, empowering people to see the world the way they want to.

## Vision

A future of eyewear that fuses cutting-edge technology with personal style, redefining the industry and empowering individuals to express their unique vision of the world.

## The four brand pillars

**Innovation** — We push the boundaries of eyewear technology, blending style and functionality with cutting-edge science to deliver smart, adaptive products that enhance our customers' daily lives.

**Style & Versatility** — Combining sophisticated design with versatility, every pair of Chamelo glasses integrates seamlessly into your lifestyle while remaining effortlessly stylish in any setting.

**Personalization** — Each pair of Chamelo eyewear is designed to fit your lifestyle. Across our collections, we offer sunglasses that reflect your personal style and preferences.

**Community** — We foster a sense of belonging, connecting with customers and creating spaces, online and offline, where innovation, feedback, and ideas are shared.

## What problem we solve

The Chamelo customer has been frustrated by static lenses — squinting through the wrong tint, carrying two pairs, missing crucial visibility during a run, ride, or commute. They want technology that's invisible in use and obvious in benefit.

Static lenses are a compromise. Chamelo removes that compromise.

## Where Chamelo wins vs. competitors

Unlike Oakley and Ray-Ban, which rely on fixed or auto-reactive tints, Chamelo gives direct, on-demand tint control — tap or slide to choose your level rather than waiting for lenses to catch up.

Unlike Transitions lenses, Chamelo is built into sporty, lifestyle-driven frames designed to look good off the field as well as on it. Chamelo delivers the "one-frame" solution regular and transition sunglasses can't.
