## What it is

Italian acetate smart eyewear with the same dual-control technology as Lion City — same HVL™ tint range, same open-ear Bluetooth audio — but in a bolder rectangular silhouette. Designed for the customer who wants Lion City's tech in a more assertive shape.

The tortoise colorway is a new acetate release as part of the Tortoise Trio collection.
