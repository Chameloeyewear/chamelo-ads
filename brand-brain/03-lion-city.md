## What it is

Italian acetate frames with electronic tint control and built-in Bluetooth audio. Square silhouette with beveled edges and metal accent details. The premium anchor of Chamelo's lineup — designed to read as premium eyewear first, tech second.

Lion City is also the world's first electronic tint-changing prescription sunglasses — the only frame in the line that's currently available with Rx.

## Controls

- **Power on:** open temples (blue LED 1 sec, then blue/red alternating)
- **Power off:** close temples 3+ seconds (red LED flashes 3x)
- **Tint:** single tap right temple (cycles through 4 levels, 48% to 8%)
- **Bluetooth pairing:** open temples, search for "Lion City"
- **Clear pairing:** tap right temple 5x quickly
- **Play/pause:** single tap left temple
- **Previous track:** double tap left temple
- **Next track:** triple tap left temple
- **Answer/end call:** single tap left temple
- **Reject call:** long press left temple (2 seconds)

## Prescription program — Lion City Rx

The world's first electronic tint-changing prescription sunglasses. Tint-changing only (not color-changing — color-changing Rx lives on Aura Rx).

- **Lens type:** single vision
- **SPH:** ±1.00 to ±7.00
- **CYL:** up to ±3.00 (Rx kit notes ±2.00 elsewhere — confirm before publishing)
- **Base curve:** 2
- **Available high-index:** 1.67 MR7 (standard) and 1.74 MR174 (for SPH ±5.00 and above)
- **Manufacturing lead time:** 2–3 weeks
- **Not currently supported:** progressives, bifocals, prism corrections

Why it matters in copy: photochromic prescription lenses depend on UV — they fail behind windshields, lag on cloudy days, and shift unpredictably with temperature. Lion City Rx puts the tint under the wearer's control instead.
