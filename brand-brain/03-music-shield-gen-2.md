## Lens — Eclipse™ Tint-Adjusting

- Light transmission: **63% to 17% VLT** (4 levels)
- Adjustment speed: **0.1 seconds** (instant)
- Control: manual slider on right temple
- Lens material: PC with liquid crystal film
- UV: **100% UVA/UVB protection** at all tint levels
- Activation: automatic when arms are opened (no button press required)
- Polarized: NO. For polarized, refer customers to Dusk.

---

## Audio

- Bluetooth: **5.3**
- Speakers: 2x open-ear drivers (directional projection)
- Microphone: 1x digital
- Voice assistant: not supported
- Audio playback: **6 hours** at 70% volume
- Call time: **5 hours**

## Battery

- Type: Lithium polymer, 3.7V
- Capacity: 150 mAh
- Tint adjustment: **62 hours continuous use**
- Standby: up to **1 year**
- Charge to 80%: ~50 minutes
- Full charge: ~1.5 hours
- Cycle durability: **500+ full charge cycles**
- Charging: magnetic pogo pin on glasses

## Controls

- **Power on:** open right temple arm
- **Power off:** close right temple arm
- **Tint:** slide control on right temple (4 levels)
- **Bluetooth pairing:** long-press button on right temple
- **Play/pause:** single click right temple button
- **Previous track:** double click right temple button
- **Answer/end call:** single click during incoming call
- **Reject call:** double click during incoming call
