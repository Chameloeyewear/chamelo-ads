## What it is

The lightest and most accessible frame in the Tortoise Trio collection. Touch-controlled smart sunglasses at the $199 entry price. Fashion-first silhouette with HVL™ tint technology — no audio, focused entirely on the lens benefit and design.

Strategic role: Zurix is Chamelo's lead product into the women's market — a segment the brand has not historically sold well to. The fashion-first positioning, accessible price, and tortoise variant in the Trio launch are all built around expanding into this audience.

## Controls

- **Power on:** open temples (green LED for 1 second, then transparent lens)
- **Power off:** close temples (red LED flashes 3 times)
- **Tint:** touch right temple to cycle through 4 levels (clear → light → medium → dark)

## LED indicators

- Charging: red LED solid
- Fully charged: green LED solid
- Low battery: red LED flashes 3 consecutive times every 5 seconds
