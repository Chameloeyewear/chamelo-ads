01

Brand Intelligence

What Chamelo actually sells beneath the apparent product. Pulled from 01_brand-overview.md and 02_voice-guide.md.

Chamelo doesn't sell sunglasses. It sells control — over light, over environment, over how you see the world.

The Chamelo customer has been frustrated by static lenses — squinting through the wrong tint, carrying two pairs, missing visibility on a run, ride, or commute. Static lenses are a compromise. Chamelo removes the compromise.

For Music Shield Gen 2, the tint slider is the hero. Audio is a supporting benefit, not the lead. The wedge is the wearer's state, not the spec: Run light. Hear everything.

The product

Tint-adjustable smart sunglasses with open-ear audio. Slide from bright to blackout in 0.1 seconds. Built for miles, not couch time.

The audience

Runners, riders, commuters. Owns one pair too many. Already convinced about smart wearables — needs proof the lens earns its place on their face every day.

The wedge

Direct, on-demand tint control. Not auto-reactive. Not Transitions. You decide. Tap or slide. The lens keeps up.
